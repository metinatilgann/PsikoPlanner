## Full Description (4000 characters)
PsikoPlanner is a professional practice management application designed specifically for therapists and psychological counselors. Securely manage your clients, sessions, and anamnesis records.

CLIENT MANAGEMENT
- 5-step detailed client registration wizard
- Personal information, demographics, health summary, family info, and therapy history
- Client search and filtering
- Archive and restore functionality

ANAMNESIS (INTAKE FORM)
- Family history
- Medical history
- Psychological history
- Medication tracking
- Presenting problem and therapy goals

SESSION MANAGEMENT
- Calendar view for session scheduling
- SOAP format session notes
- Session evaluations and risk tracking
- Individual, couple, family, and group session types

SECURITY & PRIVACY
- All client data stored locally on your device
- No health data sent to cloud servers
- Biometric authentication support
- Auto-lock feature

STATISTICS & TRACKING
- Daily session summary
- Active client count
- Weekly and monthly revenue tracking
- Upcoming session notifications

ADDITIONAL FEATURES
- Turkish and English language support
- Light and dark theme options
- Backup and restore
- Professional and user-friendly interface

PsikoPlanner helps you manage your professional practice more efficiently. All your data is securely stored on your device.
