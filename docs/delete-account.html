Hesabinizi Nasil Silebilirsiniz?

Yontem 1: Uygulama Icinden (Tavsiye Edilen)

  1. PsikoPlanner uygulamasini acin
  2. Ayarlar sekmesine gidin
  3. "Hesabimi Sil" secenegine tiklatin
  4. Onay ekraninda "Evet, hesabimi sil" secenegini onaylayin

Yontem 2: E-posta ile

Asagidaki adrese hesabinizla iliskili e-posta adresinizden bir silme talebi gonderin:

psikoplanner@gmail.com

Talebiniz en gec 7 is gunu icinde islenecektir.

Hesap Silindiginde Ne Olur?

Dikkat: Hesap silme islemi geri alinamaz.

Silinen Veriler:

  • Kimlik dogrulama verileri: E-posta ve oturum bilgileri Supabase sunucularindan kalici olarak silinir
  • Cihaz uzerindeki veriler: Uygulamayi kaldirdiginizda tum yerel veriler (danisan bilgileri, seans notlari, anamnez kayitlari) otomatik olarak silinir
Bilgi: Danisan verileriniz (kisisel bilgiler, saglik verileri, seans notlari) zaten yalnizca cihazinizda saklanmaktadir ve hicbir sunucuya gonderilmemistir. Uygulamayi silmek bu verileri kalici olarak kaldirır.

Saklama Suresi:

  • Kimlik dogrulama verileri: Silme talebinden itibaren en gec 30 gun icinde kalici olarak silinir
  • Yerel veriler: Uygulama kaldirildigi anda aninda silinir

Iletisim

Sorulariniz icin: psikoplanner@gmail.com

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: From the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the PsikoPlanner app
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Tap "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm by tapping "Yes, delete my account" on the confirmation screen

Method 2: Via Email

Send a deletion request from the email address associated with your account to:

psikoplanner@gmail.com

Your request will be processed within 7 business days.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted?

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Authentication data: Your email and session information will be permanently deleted from Supabase servers
  • On-device data: All local data (client information, session notes, anamnesis records) is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
Note: Your client data (personal information, health records, session notes) is stored exclusively on your device and has never been sent to any server. Uninstalling the app permanently removes this data.

Retention Period:

  • Authentication data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of the deletion request
  • Local data: Deleted immediately when the app is uninstalled
